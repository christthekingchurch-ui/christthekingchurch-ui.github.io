Our History

Christ the King Church has been a beacon of faith, serving the community in Iruthyapuram for several decades. Founded with the vision of spreading love, compassion, and service, the church has been at the heart of numerous transformative events. From its humble beginnings to its present-day prominence, our church's story is a testament to the unwavering faith of the community.

Through our rich history, we have hosted numerous events, worship services, and community outreach programs, all with the mission of spreading God's love. As we continue to grow, we honor our past and look forward to a brighter future, strengthened by faith and unity.
